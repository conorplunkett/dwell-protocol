Launch plan

Points now. Token at launch.

Two phases, stated plainly:

  • Phase 1 — now. You earn AIAD points: 1,000 points = $1.00 of earned ad value. Every campaign's $90-per-$100 tranche is escrowed in a USDC reserve, so points are 1:1 dollar-backed.
  • At token launch. Points convert to the AIAD token under published rules.
  • Phase 2. The reserve executes the market buys, campaigns carry per-campaign locked token rates, claims move onchain, and you can cash out AIAD → USDC via licensed partners.
