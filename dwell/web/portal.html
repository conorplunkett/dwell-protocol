Claim $DWELL

Available at token launch
  1. Claim $DWELL to your wallet. Your dwells convert to DWELL tokens at the published rate — no fee on the claim; it happens onchain, on Solana, into an email-based wallet created for you or one you connect yourself.
  2. Spend or hold. Claimed $DWELL is yours onchain — hold it, swap it, or move it anywhere.
  3. Redemptions revalue. After launch, gift cards and cash-outs are valued at the market price at redemption instead of the earn basis shown today.

Dwells themselves are non-transferable; redeeming spends them and is recorded on the ledger like every other entry.

There's nothing to connect yet. When the DWELL token launches, this chip becomes your wallet — claims happen onchain, on Solana:

  • Or connect your own — use an existing wallet with the portal instead.
