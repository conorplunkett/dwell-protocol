dwells — what earners get

1,000 dwells = $1.00 of earned ad value. Fixed. A dwell is a dollar-denominated ledger credit earned per qualified view. dwells are reward credits: non-transferable, not the token, and they never convert to $DWELL. There is no snapshot, no conversion price, and no airdrop claim.

What $DWELL is — and is not

The token does exactly two things: it trades, and it can pay for ad campaigns at the USD campaign price via a spot quote at checkout — tokens received that way go to the company treasury and are held there. A $DWELL-paid campaign is otherwise identical to a USD-paid one: viewers earn dwells, denominated in dollars.

Not equity$DWELL does not represent any share of, or claim on, the company. The Bedrock structure below is a set of constraints on the founder — it confers zero rights on token holders.
No revenue flowsThe company does not buy $DWELL with ad revenue and commits to no buybacks, burns, or distributions.
Not redeemable$DWELL is not redeemable with the company for cash, USDC, dwells, ads previously purchased, or anything else.
No bridgedwells never convert to $DWELL.

Risks

Token price volatility

$DWELL floats with the market — down to zero if the market goes there. It receives no revenue flows and no support from the company. Your earned dwells are unaffected: they are dollar-denominated and settle in USDC or Claude credits regardless of the token's price.

Thin liquidity

The Meteora pool is seeded with 60% of supply plus raise proceeds; early pools are still shallow, and large trades move the price. The company does not trade against or into the pool.

Wash-impression farming

A cash-redeemable reward raises fraud stakes. Defenses: server-authoritative impression tokens, a 2-second dwell backstop, per-device and per-IP daily caps — plus payout requires a logged-in, wallet-linked account.

Reserve adequacy

Outstanding dwells are dollar liabilities. They are backed by the earmarked rewards-pool ledger, published on the public accounting page; redemptions pause if the attestation ever fails to reconcile.
