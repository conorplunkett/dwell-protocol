What DWELL is

DWELL shows one sponsored line while a web AI assistant (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini) is generating a response, and pays the person watching in dwells — the protocol's earning unit. Dwells are dollar-denominated reward credits (1,000 dwells = $1.00 of earned ad value), redeemable for USDC to a linked wallet or Claude credits. Dwells are not the $DWELL token and do not convert to it. The Service does not read, transmit, or store the contents of your prompts or the assistant's output.
