Launch plan

Points now. Token later, under published rules.

Two phases, stated plainly:

  • Now. You earn DWELL points: 1,000 points = $1.00 of earned ad value. Every campaign's $90-per-$100 tranche is escrowed in a USDC reserve, so points are 1:1 dollar-backed.
  • At token launch. The reserve executes the market buys and points convert to DWELL tokens under published rules. From then on, each new campaign's buy locks that campaign's token rate per view.
  • After launch. You claim onchain (Solana) and can swap DWELL to USDC or withdraw via licensed partners.

The conversion rules will be published before launch and applied as written. Nothing on this page is a price promise.

FAQ
Is this crypto?

Not yet, and never on the advertiser side. Advertisers pay fixed dollar prices by card. Today you earn points backed by escrowed USDC. At token launch, points convert to the DWELL token under published rules and claims move onchain (Solana).

Do I need a wallet?

Not today. Points accrue to your DWELL account with no wallet involved. A wallet is only needed at token launch, when you claim onchain — and licensed partners handle the swap to USDC if you just want dollars out.

What are points worth?

1,000 points = $1.00 of earned ad value. Every campaign's $90-per-$100 tranche is escrowed in a USDC reserve when the advertiser pays, so points are 1:1 dollar-backed at the moment you earn them.

How do I cash out?

At token launch: points convert to DWELL tokens under the published rules, you claim onchain (Solana), and you can swap to USDC or withdraw via licensed partners. Before launch, your balance stays as dollar-backed points in the portal.

What happens at token launch?

The reserve executes the market buys and points convert under rules published ahead of time. From launch onward, each new campaign's buy locks that campaign's token rate per view — the rate you see is the rate you get for those views.

Is my earning guaranteed?

Points you have already earned are matched 1:1 by escrowed USDC. How much you earn depends on how many sponsored views you actually watch — there is no fixed payout, and nothing here is a price promise about the token.

What does the advertiser see?

A fixed dollar CPM, paid by card, for one moderated line of text with a link. Advertisers never touch points or tokens; the earn side is funded from the escrowed 90% of their spend.
